How well do I understand myself? Do I have depression or anxiety? Should I consult a therapist or seek psychiatric treatment? These tests can help answer these questions in just a few minutes, providing insight into your psychological condition and guiding you towards a better life.

All information on this platform, website, or app, including self-assessment or questionnaire results, should be discussed with a qualified healthcare professional before drawing any conclusions about your mental health. Self-assessments are not intended to provide a formal diagnosis or medical advice. Confirm any information obtained from this assessment (and any other information on this platform) with your healthcare provider, and never ignore professional advice or delay treatment because of information accessed here.
